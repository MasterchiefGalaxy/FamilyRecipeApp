Pate Hale's Booyah Chunky Soup

Ingredients:

  • 2 tsp olive oil
  • 2.5 lb stew meat
  • 2.5 lb boneless chicken thighs cut up into chunks
  • 1 large onion chopped
  • 1 large green pepper
  • 2-3 jalapeno peppers chopped
  • 3 carrots sliced
  • 2 celery ribs chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic chopped
  • 8 cups chicken stock or beef stock
  • 28 oz crushed tomatoes
  • hot sauce to taste
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1.5 lb diced potatoes
  • 2 cups shredded cabbage
  • 1.5 cups corn
  • 1.5 cups peas
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1/4 cup worcestershire sauce
  • Salt and Pepper to taste
  • Oregano to taste
  • Garlic powder to taste
  • Marjoram to taste
  • Mushrooms as needed
  • 1 sweet potato
  • Okra to taste

Instructions:

  1. Heat oil in large pot and season the beef and chicken with salt and pepper. Sear them in batches to brown them. Remove and set aside
  2. Reduce heat and add onion, peppers, carrots, and celery. Cook 5 minutes to soften. Stir in the garlic and cook another minute until the garlic becomes fragrent.
  3. Add the chicken stock, crushed tomatoes, and hot sauce to taste and add the bay leaves. Return the browned meats to the pot. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Break apart any large chunks of meat with a fork
  5. Add potatoes, cabbage, corn, peas, green beans, and worchestershire sauce. Simmer until the potatoes soften and cook through.
