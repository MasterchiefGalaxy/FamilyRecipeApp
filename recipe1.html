John's Chili

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 lb ground beef or bison
  • 1 can chili magic beans
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 tbsp Worcestershire sauce
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke
  • 1 lb Conecuh sausage
  • 4 tbsp ancho chili powder
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Instructions:

  1. Brown the ground beef or bison and sausage along with the onion.
  2. Add the diced tomatoes and chili magic beans.
  3. Stir in Worcestershire sauce, liquid smoke, ancho chili powder, and minced garlic.
  4. Simmer for 4 hours.
