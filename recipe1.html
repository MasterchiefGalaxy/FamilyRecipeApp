John's Chili

Ingredients:

  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 lb ground beef or bison
  • 1 can chili magic beans
  • 1 diced onion
  • 1 tbp worchestershire
  • 1 tsp liquid smoke
  • 1 lb conechuh sausage
  • 4 tbsp ancho chili powder
  • 3 cloves garlic
  • 1 tsp cinnamon
  • salt and pepper to taste

    • Instructions:

  • Brown the ground bison/beef and sausage along with the onion. Add the remaining ingredients and set on simmer for 4 hours.
