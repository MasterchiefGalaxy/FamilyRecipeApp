Chicken Chow Mein Skillet

Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1 lb boneless chicken breast cut into bite sized pieces
  • 1 cup carrots sliced
  • 1/2 cup celery sliced
  • 1/2 cup bell pepper chopped
  • 1/2 cup onion chopped
  • 1 cup chicken broth
  • 3 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tsp sugar
  • 1/2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 tsp fresh grated ginger root
  • 1/4 tsp lemon pepper
  • salt to taste
  • 2 tbsp corn starch
  • 4 cups fresh bean sprouts
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 1 cup chow mein noodles

Instructions:

  1. Cook chicken in oil and then add all ingredients besides the sprouts
  2. Bring to a boil, then reduce heat and simmer for 5 minutes uncovered
  3. Stir in sprouts and cook another 2-4 minutes
  4. In a small bowl mix cornstarch with water and stir until dissolved
  5. Add to skillet mixture and stir well until thickened. Then serve over chow mein noodles
