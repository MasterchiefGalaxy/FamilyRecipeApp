Pate Hale's Eagle Brand Peanut Butter Fudge

Ingredients:

  • 12 oz pkg white morsels white chocolate chips
  • 1 can eagle brand sweetened condensed milk
  • 3/4 cup peanut butter

Instructions:

  1. Line a 8x8 pan with parchment paper and spray with cooking spray
  2. Pour all the milk and white morsels and peanut butter into a microwavable safe bowl and nuke for 1 minute
  3. Stir the mixture and return it to the microwave and heat for another minute
  4. Remove from microwave and pour into prepared pan
  5. Put the pan in the refrigerator so the fudge will set quickly
  6. Cut into squares
