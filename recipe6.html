Pat Hale's Chicken with Mushrooms and Swiss Cheese

Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless skinless chicken breast halves
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup crushed ritz crackers
  • 3/4 tsp salt to taste
  • pepper to tast
  • 1 container of fresh mushrooms, sliced
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 4 slices of ham
  • 4 slices of swiss cheese

Instructions:

  1. Flatten chicken to 1/4 thickness and place eggs in a shallow bowl
  2. Combine cracker crumbs, salt, and pepper in another bowl
  3. Dip the chicken in eggs, then in the cracker crumbs and set aside
  4. In a large ovenproof skillet, saute the mushrooms in 2 tbsp butter until tender, remove and set aside
  5. Add the remaining butter, melt and add chicken. Cook over medium heat for 3-4 minutes on each side.
  6. Top the chicken with a slice of ham, mushrooms and cheese slices, then broil for 1-2 minutes until cheese melts
